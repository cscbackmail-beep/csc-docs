Colombo Swimming Club
Is This the Governance
You Want for the Club?
When a President alters official minutes, overrules the General Secretary, and puts staff under surveillance — members need to ask what kind of organisation they belong to.
The General Secretary prepared accurate minutes from audio recordings — her constitutional duty. The President overruled her, altered the minutes, and had his version circulated instead.
The General Manager was directed to take over the recording and preparation of minutes — a role outside his operational function — and worked under surveillance with no space to act freely.
Staff were placed under pressure. Transparency was disregarded. The Constitution and proper processes were ignored entirely.
The GS-prepared minutes of the 5th and 15th May meetings have still not been circulated to members. Members are being kept in the dark.
Members are entitled to ask
Why are the original minutes being suppressed?
What was said at those meetings that someone does not want members to know?
Is this the governance standard we accept for our Club?

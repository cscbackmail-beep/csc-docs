Colombo Swimming Club
You Have a Right
to See the Documents
Under Article 12A of the Constitution, every member is entitled to access Club records and financial documents. In connection with this matter, you are entitled to request and receive:
  • All fee proposals, quotations and invoices submitted by the legal service providers
  • All Procurement Sub-Committee records — or evidence that no such process took place
  • All General Committee minutes and resolutions relating to this engagement
  • All correspondence and instructions issued to the legal service providers
  • All documents evidencing that this expenditure is a proper liability of the Club
If you have not seen these documents, ask for them.
The Club is constitutionally obliged to provide them.
Informed Members. Stronger Club.

Colombo Swimming Club — Post 19
150 People Have Been Waiting.
Some Since 2019.
As of May 2026, 150 applications remain pending — the oldest dating to 2019. Of those called for interview in 2025, the earliest application was from 2023. Nobody who applied before 2023 has been called.
The Constitution gives the General Secretary — not the President — the power to scrutinise applications and call applicants for interview. The waiting list determines the order.
The President's own email of 20 May 2026 now commits to processing applications "in the chronological order in which the applications were originally received." If that was always the rule, why say it now?

The queue existed. It was not respected.

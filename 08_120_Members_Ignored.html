Why Has an SGM
Not Been Held?
120 members formally requisitioned a Special General Meeting. This is a constitutional right — not a request, not a suggestion.
The meeting has not been convened. No credible explanation has been given to members for the delay or refusal.
A Special General Meeting is not conflict. It is democracy. Whether you agree or disagree with the requisition, every member should support the principle that members deserve a voice.
A strong Club listens to its members.
Silencing 120 of them is not governance. It is the opposite.
Our Club. Our Voice.

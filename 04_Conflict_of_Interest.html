Conflict of Interest
The President is reportedly one of the individuals for whose personal benefit these legal services were obtained.
He is simultaneously a Trustee, a General Committee member, and a direct beneficiary of the proposed payment — on both sides of the same transaction.
The Constitution is explicit: no Committee member may allow personal interest to take precedence over the Club's. A conflict must be declared and the member must withdraw from all related decisions.
Members should be asking: has this happened?

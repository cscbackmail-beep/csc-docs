Is This Even
a Club Expense?
The legal fees — reportedly Rs. 2 Million — were incurred defending certain individual General Committee members named in legal proceedings. Not the Club itself.
The Constitution contains no provision authorising Club funds to be used for the personal legal defence of individual committee members.
Nor does it appear to authorise the Club to retrospectively assume liabilities personally incurred by such individuals.
Before a single rupee is paid — is this the Club's liability, or theirs?
Club Funds Belong to All Members.

Colombo Swimming Club
What Members
Should Be Demanding
No payment of legal fees should be authorised until:
The Audit Committee has independently examined the entire engagement and reported its findings to the Membership
An independent legal opinion has been obtained confirming this is a legitimate Club expense
Full disclosure has been made to members on who was represented, why, and on what constitutional basis Club funds are proposed to be used
The matter has been placed before a General Meeting for member approval
These are not unreasonable demands.
They are what the Constitution requires. Members should insist on nothing less.

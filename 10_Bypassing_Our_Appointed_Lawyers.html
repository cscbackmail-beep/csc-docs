Colombo Swimming Club
Why Was Our Own
Law Firm Set Aside?
The Club has appointed lawyers — Julius & Creasy — a reputed firm engaged to handle the Club's legal matters. They were not asked to act in these proceedings.
Instead, lawyers with reported personal connections to the President appear to have been engaged — at reportedly far higher fees.
No competitive selection. No procurement process. No transparency. The Constitution requires all three.
Members are entitled to ask: why was the Club's own law firm set aside — and who made that call?
Questions are not disloyalty. They are responsibility.

Colombo Swimming Club — Post 13
She Recorded the Truth.
He Ordered it Buried.
The General Secretary (GS) audio-recorded the GC meetings of 5th and 15th May 2026 in her official capacity and prepared minutes from those recordings — her constitutional duty. The President altered both sets of minutes, removing her statements, and had his versions circulated instead. Three GC members signed the revised versions.
The President instructed the General Manager and Executive Secretary — by email and telephone — not to circulate the original minutes. The GS formally objected and refused to circulate the altered versions. At the 25th May meeting, the GS was prevented from performing her duties and the GM took over minute-taking at the President's direction.
The matter was escalated to the Additional Trustees and Audit Committee on 5th June 2026. The Constitution requires minutes to be circulated within 30 days. That deadline has now elapsed. The GS-prepared minutes of the 5th and 15th May meetings have still not been circulated to members. Both versions remain available at the Administration Office under Clause 12(a).
